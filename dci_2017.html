2017 Finals Recap

Rank Corps Finals Score General Effect Visual Music
1 The Blue Devils 98.538 39.675 29.700 29.163
2 Santa Clara Vanguard 97.600 39.150 29.150 29.300
3 Carolina Crown 96.575 38.500 28.900 29.175
4 The Cavaliers 95.200 38.150 28.600 28.450
5 The Bluecoats 95.163 38.250 28.450 28.463

2017, DCI's 45th season, had a relatively wide range of scores among the top three, while having a razor thin race for fourth place. By mid-season, the Blue Devils established a clear win, topping every show for the last month. While Sanata Clara Vanguard started in fifth place, they rose to second by the mid-season and remained there through finals. Carolina Crown stuck mostly to the center, and the Cavaliers rounded out the top five most of the season. The most significant movement was from the Bluecaots, who started in first place, but slowed down in the mid-season and eventually slipped to fifth.
