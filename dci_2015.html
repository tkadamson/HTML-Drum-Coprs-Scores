2015 Finals Recap

Rank Corps Finals Score General Effect Visual Music
1 The Blue Devils 97.650 38.850 29.400 29.400
2 Carolina Crown 97.075 38.950 28.950 29.175
3 The Bluecoats 96.925 38.800 29.000 29.125
4 The Cadets 95.900 38.200 28.450 29.250
5 Santa Clara Vanguard 93.850 37.150 28.200 28.500

The 2015 season ended with a photo finish, with less than one point separating the top three corps. Ultimately, the Blue Devils caption wins in Visual and Music put them over the top. Carolina Crown had a weak opening, and was in fifth place mid-season. But changes to their closer in July increaqrsed their General Effect score to produce a fan-favorite show. The Cadets started out the season strong, but while they plateaued in the last week, The Bluecoats rose to capture the third-place spot. While Vanguard also had a strong early season, they didn't rise as much as the other four in the late-season.
